Biography

"Based in Europe, I have been composing music for 15 years, with Japanese role-playing games as my primary focus. My work also extends to other game genres and media that benefit from dynamic melodies and strong atmospheres, always aiming to create an engaging experience for the listener.

My deepest inspiration comes from the games of the PlayStation 1 era, especially Japanese RPGs, which first sparked my passion for storytelling through game development, visuals, and music. Long before I began composing, music shaped my story ideas and visual concepts, strengthening my understanding of how sound and imagery intertwine. When I transitioned to composing, this foundation became a valuable asset, complementing my formal training and continuous practice in composition and sound design.

While my music is rooted in the fantasy and storytelling traditions of RPGs, I also enjoy exploring different genres and tools to bring new dimensions to my compositions.
